Wanna see the emotions of your interviewee?

We present our solution for HR departments of any organization who wants to get the best candidates. Our solution will help you to save a lot of time and money by analyzing the emotions of interwee for you! START NOW

Which are the greatest things about it?

Easy in use You don't need to understand how emotion classification. Just upload the video and look at intuitive results in the form of infographics.
End-to-end application You don't need to convert your videos or separate the audio from video and submit text. Our application does everything for you!
Multi-modal approach Our main model utilizes all of the benefits of a multi-modal approach and recognizes emotions from audio, video and text.
